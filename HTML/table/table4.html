Chocolate Chip Cookies

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Steps

  1. Mix the Dough -Combine butter, sugar, and eggs in a bowl. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. Mix until a smooth dough forms.
  2. Shape the Cookies -Scoop small portions of dough, roll them into balls, and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Flatten slightly.
  3. Bake to Perfection -Preheat the oven to 180oC (350oF) and bake for 10-12 minutes until the edges are set. Let them cool before enjoying!

Incredients

Incredients Quality
Flour 1/2 Cups
Unsweetened cocoa powder 1/2 Cups
Butter (softened) 1/2 Cups (1 Stick)
Vanilla extract 1 Teaspoon
Chocolate chips 3/4 Cups
